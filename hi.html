​VOICES FROM ANOTHER WORLD

Rabbi Sholem Perl · VOICES FROM ANOTHER WORLD
Meet Reuvain, a wealthy miser who evaded certain death. Listen to how Rabbi Perl, Master Storyteller, keeps you in suspense as Reuvain learns a powerful lesson, when his luxurious life takes a tumultuous turn, enduring a year of homelessness and poverty.

Lesson: We learn the importance of Hachnosas Orchim - inviting guests into our home and caring and providing for the poor and needy.

THE SIDDUR FLIES TO HEAVEN

Rabbi Sholem Perl · THE SIDDUR FLIES TO HEAVEN
The decree of 1827 conscripted and forced very young Jewish boys from age 7 into the Czar's Army, for as long as 25 years. Fasten your seatbelts as Rabbi Sholem Perl, master storyteller, keeps you at the edge of your seat, as you experience the suspenseful moment the “Cantonist” child stood steadfast to his Yiddishkeit under tremendous pressure, and sends a siddur to Shomayim - Heaven, as he defies the Czar's General’s orders.
​Lesson: To stay proud, strong and committed to Yiddishkeit.
​

TZITZIS SAVES A LIFE

Rabbi Sholem Perl · TZITZIS SAVES A LIFE
Tune in and listen to Rabbi Perl, Master Storyteller, transport you back in time and witness a young boy Shmuel escape from the death train on the way to the Auschwitz concentration camp.  
Experience how choosing to do a Mitzvah in a very challenging time, having Mesiras Nefesh, actually saved Shmuli’s life.
​Lesson: To cherish wearing Tzitzis even when it’s uncomfortable.
​Child's Voice: Pesach Newman of Cheder Menachem, Los Angeles 

TRAPPED IN A VEGETABLE

Rabbi Sholem Perl · TRAPPED IN A VEGETABLE
“STOP!!! Don’t drink that vodka”! exclaimed the Baal Shem Tov. Listen while Rabbi Perl, Master Storyteller, transports you in time, to the Bal Shem Tov. See how the Baal Shem Tov helps release a trapped Neshama - a soul, so it can reach its destination in Gan Eden - Heaven, through saying a Bracha - a blessing on a drink.

​Lesson: The importance of saying a Bracha - blessing on food & drink. You may never know whose Neshama-soul you are elevating.

THE SPEECHLESS TALMID

​NEVER BE AFRAID - BE A SCHNEERSONKI

Rabbi Sholem Perl · NEVER BE AFRAID - BE A SCHNEERSONKI
Rabbi Perl Master Storyteller will keep you fascinated while traveling to frigid Siberia to meet  the Chosid Reb Yechiel Rappoport imprisoned there, toiling for 16 years of backbreaking outdoor labor. 
Reb Yechiel Rappoport remained strong and steadfast to his Yiddishkeit, his faith and his trust in Hashem, knowing that he is always in the Hands of Hashem, who loves him dearly.

​Lesson: The importance of remaining strong to our Yiddishkeit, even if our faith is tested. Just as the Chossid of the Frierdiker Rebbe, Reb Yechiel Rappoport lived his life mission of ‘ Never to be afraid’ we too know “never to be afraid”, because we are in the Hands of Hashem, who loves us dearly.

​REB AMNON OF MAINZ

Rabbi Sholem Perl · Reb Amnon - The Power Of Mesiras Nefesh
This theatrical story winds your clock back to the 12th century, when Rabbi Amnon of Mainz begged “Please give me three days”. Witness the emotion in which he wrote the famous and holy Tefilah - Unesaneh Tokef.  Join Master Storyteller Rabbi Sholem Perl on this unforgettable journey.
Lesson: We have to follow in the ways of our Holy Tzadikim who lived with Mesiras Nefesh. Today it means to let go of our negative and self centered desires and spend time helping others and connecting to Hashem.
